Hi there! Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Barbara Rosa I’m an illustrator and Background Developer, currently based in Portugal. I just recently graduated from the University of Sunderland in the UK.

Most of my work is digital, weather on Photoshop and/or Procreate. I also do tradition work, with watercolour, colour pencils and pen. Most of my work is for babies, children, and younger viewers! Even though I do try to challenge myself with all types of work, I’m open-minded when it comes to working. All of my personal work is based on my imagination, using as reference a lot of what’s surrounding me such as trees, people, feelings.


Just like everyone else I do find myself solving issues I get a long way with work, but with creativity and problem solving I always find the best solution.

Please feel free to contact me about exciting work opportunities! Big and small projects alike are very welcome.
